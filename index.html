Personal Commercial Project

Station-31

Station-31 is a first-person sci-fi suspense game set inside an unregistered space station. The player explores the station, aligns antennas, sends signals, and slowly discovers that something hidden is interfering with communication.

  • Designed a story-focused exploration loop without combat.
  • Focused on atmosphere, environmental storytelling, and tension.
  • Planned as a short Steam demo with a strong first 30 minutes.

The Deep Market

A satirical first-person supermarket game set in a dystopian Netherlands dominated by major supermarket chains. The player shops under a limited budget while dealing with time pressure, obstacles, deals, and shopping accuracy.

  • Built pickup, drop, cart, shopping list, timer, and score systems.

  • Balanced realism, satire, and readable gameplay goals.
